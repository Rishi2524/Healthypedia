How to meditate for self discovery

Co-authored by Soken Garf

Last updated: 20th, September 2023 Approved

When you’re looking for ways to get to know the “true you,” meditation is a powerful practice of mindfulness
you can try to help you along your path to self-discovery. In general, meditation promotes self-discovery by
letting you focus all your attention inwards and blocking out external distractions. Once you’re looking inwards
there are certain ways you can focus your mediation sessions that can help you get closer to your true self.
Follow the steps on this list to give it a shot.
